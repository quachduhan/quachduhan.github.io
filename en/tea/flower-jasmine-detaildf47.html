Jasmine Blossom Tea

Jasmine Blossom Tea is one of the most favorite in the art of the tea ceremony in Vietnam and the Asia area.
Call for price
Description

Jasmine Blossom Tea is hand made from Jasmine flowers enclosed by tea leaf buds. When infused in warm water, Jasmine Blossom Tea releases a ring of Jasmine flowers from the Tea buds, creating a spectacular eruption that makes your teatime a real ceremony.

Jasmine Blossom Tea, made from the best natural green tea, is the healthiest beverage. It helps to improve health and brain function, to increase fat burning, and help to lose weight. It also can lower the risk of various types of cancer, cardiovascular disease, type II diabetes, protect brain in old age people, lower the risk of Alzheimer’s and Parkinson’s. Green Tea helps you live longer and healthier.

Jasmine Blossom Tea is a type of flower tea that has characteristic aromas of Jasmine flowers combined with the fresh and pure taste of Green Tea.

Preparation:

- Put one single blossom in a transparent teapot.

- Add boiling water into the teapot, and wait three to five minutes until the blossom completely opens.

- Enjoy the tea.
